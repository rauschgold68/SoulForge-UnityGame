# SoulForge

## Description

This Unity game was developed for a university project in the module "Interactive Systems". It is a challenging rougelite dungeon crawler with souls-like aspects.

### Gameplay Loop

* **Objective:** Progress through three rooms to defeat the final boss.
* **Combat:** Players collect **Souls** from defeated enemies.
* **Permanent Progression:** Souls are used at the spawn point to permanently upgrade the power of cards within the player's deck.
* **In-Run Upgrades:** After clearing a room, the player draws three random cards from their deck and must choose one.
    * Selected cards provide temporary buffs (e.g., +150 Health or increased Attack Power).
    * Once a card is chosen, it is removed from the pool for the remainder of the run.
    * All in-run card effects reset upon death.
